Thayya — mobile hero explorations

Design review response: the dotted rangoli overpowered the hero copy on mobile. Three concepts at 390×844 (scaled). Concept C is the recommendation and is what is implemented in the product: content owns the top of the viewport, the rangoli becomes a masked bottom-edge accent, the primary call to action is full-width and dominant. Full rationale: UI-UX.md, Addendum v3.0 (A8).

Readability is guaranteed structurally (a mask), not tuned (an opacity guess) — and the desktop split layout uses the same content-column / artwork-zone model.
